About City Plumbing Services

Manchester's Plumbers Since 2009

City Plumbing Services was founded by Dave Halloran in 2009 with one simple aim: to provide a plumbing service in Greater Manchester that homeowners and landlords could genuinely rely on.

Over fifteen years and thousands of completed jobs later, that aim hasn't changed. We have grown to a team of six full-time engineers — all Gas Safe registered, all directly employed. No agency staff, no subcontractors we can't vouch for.

Every job gets the same approach regardless of size: turn up on time, do the work properly, leave the place clean, and communicate clearly throughout. It sounds obvious, but it's what sets us apart from the competition.

With over 200 five-star Google reviews from customers across Greater Manchester, the work speaks for itself.

  • 6 directly employed engineers
