Local Knowledge

Plumbing in Rochdale — What to Know

Spotland, in the OL12 postcode west of the town centre, and Castleton in OL11 to the south-east, contain dense Victorian terrace streets with a plumbing profile that includes a high proportion of back boilers still in service. These systems ran reliably for 30 or 40 years and are now well past their design life — we carry out a significant number of full back boiler-to-combi conversions in these areas every year.

Norden and Bamford, in the northern reaches of the OL12 postcode and climbing towards Ashworth Valley and the Pennines, are a different proposition. The housing here is substantially post-war and later — detached and semi-detached properties, owner-occupied, where the focus is on quality rather than emergency repair. Bathroom renovations and smart heating controls are among the most common jobs in this part of Rochdale. We understand the difference and tailor our approach to the property rather than applying a standard process regardless of context.
