Plumbing in Bury — What to Know

Radcliffe, in the BL9 postcode south of Bury town centre, has a high concentration of Victorian terrace housing where back boilers are still common. Many of these systems are operating well beyond their design life — they run, but they're inefficient, occasionally unreliable, and increasingly expensive to repair. We carry out back boiler-to-combi conversions in Radcliffe regularly and can survey a property and quote for the full job in a single visit.

Tottington, in the BL8 postcode north-west of Bury, has a predominantly semi-detached and detached housing mix from the post-war and late-twentieth-century periods.
