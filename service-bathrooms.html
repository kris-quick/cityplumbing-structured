Complete Bathroom Projects

A New Bathroom, Done Properly from Start to Finish

A bathroom renovation is one of the most rewarding things you can do in a home when it's done well. The difference between a bathroom that works beautifully and one that causes problems — leaks behind tiles, poorly draining showers, badly siliconed edges — almost always comes down to the quality of the installation. That's where 15 years of experience matters.

City Plumbing project manages the full job, coordinates the trades involved — plumbing, tiling, electrical where needed — and keeps you updated throughout. Whether you want a modern suite, a classic freestanding bath or a fully accessible wet room, we'll get it right.

  • Full bathroom renovations and refits
  • Wet room design and installation
  • En-suite conversions and new en-suites
  • Supply and fit, or fit-only if you have your own suite

How It Works

How a Bathroom Project Works

We keep the process clear and manage the work so you don't have to worry about coordinating trades.

01

Consultation

We visit, take measurements and discuss style, layout, accessibility needs and budget. You'll get a clear, itemised quote before anything is agreed.

02

Design & Supply

We help select sanitaryware, tiles, fixtures and fittings from reputable suppliers. Nothing is ordered until you're happy with the plan.

03

Installation

Strip-out, first fix plumbing, tanking and waterproofing, tiling, second fix and fitting out — all coordinated by us. We keep the site clean and respect your home.

04

Final Check & Handover

Every element is tested before handover. You'll be shown how everything works and left with a clean, finished bathroom.
