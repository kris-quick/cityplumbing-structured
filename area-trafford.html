Trafford's Local Plumbing Team

Trafford covers a wide sweep of Greater Manchester — from the dense Victorian housing of Old Trafford and Stretford through to the spacious semis and detached homes of Sale, Urmston, and Flixton. The area has a very high rate of owner-occupied family homes, and that's reflected in the kind of work we do here: boiler upgrades, central heating improvements, and bathroom renovations by homeowners who want quality work that lasts.

Old Trafford and parts of Stretford have older housing stock — Victorian and inter-war — with heating and pipework that varies enormously from house to house. In Sale and Urmston, the homes tend to be 1950s to 1980s semis and detached properties, often with systems that are overdue for an update. We know both ends of Trafford's housing spectrum and give straight advice on what's needed.

Local Knowledge

Plumbing in Trafford — What to Know

Trafford is predominantly an owner-occupier borough. Homeowners tend to invest in their properties — bathroom renovations, boiler upgrades, and central heating improvements are among our most common jobs here. The housing stock in Sale and Urmston has aged to the point where many original systems are reaching the end of their practical life.

Old Trafford's Victorian and Edwardian terraces around Shrewsbury Street and Great Stone Road often have sections of original lead supply pipe running into modern plastic connections. We check for lead pipework as a matter of course in these properties and advise on replacement where appropriate.
