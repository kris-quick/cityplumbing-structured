Repairs & Servicing

All Makes and Models, Properly Diagnosed

Boilers give warning signs before they fail completely — strange noises, dropping pressure, slow heat-up times, error codes on the display. Ignoring those signs usually means a more expensive repair further down the line. Getting a Gas Safe engineer in early often means the difference between a straightforward fix and a full replacement.

Our engineers carry extensive knowledge of the major boiler manufacturers and stock a wide range of common spare parts. We work on Worcester Bosch, Vaillant, Baxi, Ideal, Glow-worm, Potterton and most other makes found in Manchester homes.

  • Boiler breakdowns and fault diagnosis
  • Annual servicing — homeowners and landlords
  • Pressure loss, pilot light issues and error codes
  • Gas Safe registered (reg: 000000) — legally qualified to work on gas

How a Boiler Repair or Service Works

A clear process from first call to a boiler that's working safely and efficiently.

01

Fault Diagnosis
