Water Damage Doesn't Wait — Neither Do We

A burst pipe in the middle of the night or a sudden leak on Christmas Day can cause serious damage to your home in a very short space of time. The longer water is left unchecked, the more it spreads — through floorboards, into ceilings below, soaking insulation and causing structural damage that costs far more to put right than the original plumbing fault.

City Plumbing has been handling plumbing emergencies across Manchester since 2009. Our engineers are experienced, fully equipped, and ready to take your call at any hour. We'll talk you through isolating your water supply while you wait — and get to you as quickly as we can.

  • Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
  • Burst pipes, flooding and major leaks
  • No hot water or sudden boiler breakdowns
  • Gas Safe registered for gas-related emergencies

How Our Emergency Call-Out Works

A clear, straightforward process from the moment you pick up the phone.

01

Call Us

Ring 0161 000 0000 any time. Tell us what's happening and where you are. We'll advise on immediate steps like turning off your stop tap to limit damage while you wait.

02

Engineer Dispatched

We send the nearest available engineer. Our vans carry the parts and tools needed to handle the most common emergency situations without a return visit.

03

Fault Diagnosed

The engineer assesses the situation properly before touching anything. You'll know exactly what the problem is and what it will take to fix it — no surprises.

The repair is completed to a proper standard. We test everything before leaving to confirm the problem is fully resolved and your system is safe.
