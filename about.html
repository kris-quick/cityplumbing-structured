Our Story

Founded on a Simple Principle

City Plumbing Services started in 2009 when Dave Halloran — a qualified plumber with ten years of experience under his belt — decided there had to be a better way to run a plumbing business in Manchester.

The problem wasn't technical skill. There were plenty of capable plumbers around. The problem was reliability. Too many tradespeople were turning up late, not communicating, leaving jobs unfinished, or quoting one price and charging another. Dave had heard the complaints from friends, family, and neighbours too many times.

So he set out to build a plumbing company that operated the way customers actually wanted: honest quotes, confirmed appointment times, clean workmanship, and clear communication throughout. He started with just himself and a van. Within three years he had taken on two engineers. Today the team numbers six, all directly employed, all Gas Safe registered, all trained to the same standard.

The business has grown entirely on reputation and word of mouth. We have never paid for advertising. Every new customer comes through a recommendation or a Google review left by a satisfied customer. That is the kind of business we are, and the kind we intend to stay.

Over fifteen years and thousands of completed jobs across Greater Manchester, the original principle has not changed. Do the work properly. Be honest. Communicate. Leave the place clean. That is still what we are about.

How We Work

What We Stand For

These are not slogans. They are the practical standards every City Plumbing engineer is held to on every job, every day.

01

Honesty Before Anything Else

If a job does not need doing, we will tell you. If a repair is a better option than a replacement, we will say so. We make money by doing good work, not by upselling unnecessary jobs.

02

Fixed Prices, No Surprises

Every job is quoted in writing before we start. If we discover additional work is needed, we tell you what it is and what it costs before we proceed. You are always in control.

03

Confirmed Times, Not Vague Windows

We give you an appointment time and we keep to it. If something genuinely changes, we call ahead. We do not leave customers waiting around for half a day without explanation.

04

Clean Work, Clean Finish

We bring dust sheets, we take our waste with us, and we wipe up after ourselves. Your home or property should look better when we leave than when we arrived — not worse.

05

Safety Is Non-Negotiable

Every engineer who works on gas in your property is Gas Safe registered. Every installation meets current building regulations. We do not cut corners on safety for any reason.

06

Relationships Over Transactions

We would rather have a customer for fifteen years than a one-off job done in a rush. We take the time to do things properly because that is what builds the kind of business we want to run.
