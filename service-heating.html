Heating Solutions

A Warmer, More Efficient Home Starts with the Right System

A heating system that's inefficient, poorly balanced or hasn't been properly maintained will cost more to run and heat your home less effectively. Whether you're replacing an ageing system, extending into a new room, or looking to get more out of what you already have, the right approach can make a real difference to your comfort and energy bills.

City Plumbing has been installing and upgrading central heating systems across Manchester since 2009. We survey your property, design a system that works for the space, and install it properly — including balancing the radiators, commissioning the boiler and setting up the controls so the system runs as efficiently as it should.

  • New central heating system installation
  • Radiator additions, upgrades and replacements
  • Power flushing for dirty or slow-heating systems
  • Smart thermostats and underfloor heating

How It Works

How a Central Heating Installation Works

Planned carefully, installed properly, commissioned so it runs correctly from day one.

01

System Survey

We visit and assess room sizes, insulation, existing pipework, the boiler (if staying), and what the system needs to achieve. You'll get a full picture before committing.

02

Design

We size radiators correctly for each room, plan the pipework routes, and advise on controls — including whether a smart thermostat would suit the setup.

03

Installation

Pipework is run, radiators fitted, the system filled and treated with inhibitor. We work cleanly and tidy up as we go.

04

Commission & Test

Boiler set up correctly, radiators balanced, controls programmed and tested. We walk you through how everything works before we leave.
