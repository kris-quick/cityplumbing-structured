Local Knowledge

Plumbing in Stockport — What to Know

Stockport's Victorian and Edwardian housing stock — filling SK1, SK2, and SK3 around Edgeley, Heaviley, and Davenport — has a characteristic plumbing profile: lead supply pipes, gravity-fed water systems with cold water tanks in the loft, and heating systems modified over many decades. Cast iron radiators are common in these properties and can last a very long time if maintained. Sludge builds up faster in older systems — a power flush every few years can add years of life to a heating system that might otherwise need full replacement.

The SK4 postcode — covering Heaton Moor, Heaton Norris, and Reddish — has a slightly different character, with larger Victorian and Edwardian semis attracting professional households. These are often the properties where comprehensive upgrades happen: full bathroom renovation, new boiler, smart thermostat — all managed by us alongside daily life in the home.
