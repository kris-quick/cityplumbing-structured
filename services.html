Landlord Gas Safety Certificates

Legally required for all rental properties with gas appliances. We issue CP12 Gas Safety Certificates — same-day where possible — and work with landlords, letting agents, and property management companies across Greater Manchester.

  • CP12 Gas Safety Certificates (legally required annually)
  • Same-day certificate issue where possible
  • Boiler and appliance checks included
  • Multi-property landlord packages available
  • Letting agent accounts welcome
  • Electronic certificates emailed immediately on completion

Book your certificate before it expires — late certificates can invalidate your landlord insurance.
