Everyday Repairs

The Plumbing Jobs That Need Doing Properly

Small plumbing problems have a way of becoming bigger ones if they're left. A dripping tap wastes water and money. A running toilet can silently waste thousands of litres a month. A weeping joint under the sink is damaging the cabinet below even if it seems minor. These aren't emergencies, but they're worth fixing sooner rather than later.

We deal with the full range of day-to-day plumbing repairs — from replacing a washer on a leaky tap to fitting a new stop tap, sorting out water pressure issues, or installing a new unvented hot water cylinder. Whatever the job, we give you a clear price before we start.

  • Dripping taps, leaking pipes and running toilets
  • Outdoor taps, stop taps and isolation valves
  • Hot water cylinders — vented and unvented
  • Water pressure problems diagnosed and resolved

How It Works

How a General Plumbing Repair Works

Clear, straightforward — you know exactly what's happening and what it costs before we start.

01

We Assess

The engineer looks at the problem properly — not just the symptom but the cause. A running toilet could be a float valve, flush valve or fill valve issue — each needs a different fix.

02

Quote Before Starting

You'll know what the repair involves and costs before any work starts. No hidden charges. If something unexpected comes up, we stop and discuss it first.

03

Carry Out the Repair

Done properly, using the right parts. If a part needs ordering, we arrange a return visit rather than leaving something temporary.

04

Test & Confirm

Before leaving, we run the tap, flush the toilet, check the pressure — whatever we've worked on. You'll see it working before we pack up.
