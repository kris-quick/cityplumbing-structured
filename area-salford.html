Local Knowledge

Plumbing in Salford — What to Know

The MediaCityUK development transformed Salford Quays over the past fifteen years. The apartments built there — across Quayside, The Lightwell, and surrounding blocks — have pressurised hot water systems and modern combi boilers. That doesn't mean they're problem-free. Expansion vessels fail, pressure drops, and combis develop faults even when relatively new. We work in MediaCityUK properties regularly and know the systems installed there.

The older housing in Pendleton and Ordsall is a different story. Copper, iron, and plastic pipe can all be present in a single house — all installed at different points over many decades. It's not a problem if you know what you're looking at. We do, and we work safely and correctly in these properties every week across Salford.
