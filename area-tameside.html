Local Knowledge

Plumbing in Tameside — What to Know

Ashton-under-Lyne, in the OL6 and OL7 postcodes, is Tameside's main commercial centre and has some of the borough's oldest housing stock. The terraced streets around Cavendish Street and Stamford Street have typical Victorian plumbing characteristics — lead supply pipes in many cases, gravity-fed water systems, and heating that's been added, removed, and modified over many decades. These properties need a plumber who understands how the different elements interact rather than someone applying a standard approach regardless of what's actually there.

Dukinfield, between Ashton and Hyde, has a significant amount of 1970s housing stock — council-built semis and short terraces where original heating systems are now failing. We carry out a lot of full system replacements in Dukinfield and project-manage the entire job from initial survey through to commissioning and handover. Stalybridge, with its stone-built terraces and slightly elevated position, has a character all of its own — we've worked there extensively and know what these properties require.
